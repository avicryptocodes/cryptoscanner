LONGRule-based bullish setup. Structure, liquidity, and derivatives confluence support upside continuation or reversal.
SHORTRule-based bearish setup. Structure, liquidity, and derivatives confluence support downside continuation or reversal.
WAITNo clean confluence. The pair may be ranging, mixed across timeframes, or missing a valid liquidity trigger.
ScoreConfluence score from structure, multi-timeframe bias, sweeps, FVG, OI, funding, volume, and crowd-positioning signals.
FundingPerpetual funding rate. Negative means shorts are paying longs; positive means longs are paying shorts.
OIOpen interest. Rising OI can mean fresh positioning entering; falling OI can mean deleveraging or position closing.
L/SLong/short account ratio when available. High values show crowded longs; low values show crowded shorts.
TrendCurrent OHLC structure state: clean uptrend, clean downtrend, transition, or range.
BiasDirectional structure bias on the selected timeframe: bullish, bearish, or range.
BOSBreak of structure. Price has broken a recent swing high or swing low.
SweepLiquidity grab. Price trades beyond a recent high or low, then closes back inside the level.
FVGFair value gap or imbalance detected from OHLC candles. Used as a possible reaction or entry zone.
BSL / SSLBuy-side liquidity sits above swing highs. Sell-side liquidity sits below swing lows.
Entry / SLSuggested entry zone and stop-loss based on nearby liquidity, ATR, FVG, and support/resistance logic.
TP1 / TP2 / TP3Targets based on liquidity and risk expansion. TP2 is also used for displayed risk/reward.
ATRAverage true range. A volatility measure used to size stops and judge displacement strength.
MTFMulti-timeframe confluence. Selected timeframe checked against higher timeframes before ranking a setup.
Derivatives RulesOI Up + Price Up = Long. OI Up + Price Down = Short. OI Down + Price Up = Fade. OI Down + Price Down = Wait. Extreme positive funding = Short squeeze risk. Extreme negative funding = Long squeeze risk.
