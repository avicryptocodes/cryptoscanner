① Configure

Pick your Timeframe (5m–1D), Universe (USDT perps), and Depth. Full Market = all pairs; Top 20–75 = fastest scan.

② Scan

Hit Scan Market. Engine fetches live OI, funding, candles, and runs structure-first SMC analysis on every pair.

③ Filter results

Use Signal / Trend / Structure dropdowns. Sort by Score — structure drives the score, derivatives are a small bonus.

④ Open detail

Click any row to open the full execution plan — entry zone, stop, TP1/2/3, R:R, FVG, BSL/SSL, and confluence notes.

⑤ WAIT setups

A WAIT badge means no MSS/CHoCH confirmed yet. The detail panel explains exactly what's missing and what to watch for.

Use the Single Coin tab for a deep scan on any symbol at any timeframe — no full market scan needed.
